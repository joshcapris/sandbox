perspective: the intensity of your view. range from (0 - 2000)
-is only apparent when object is rotated/angled not including Z plane
- 0px is the vanishing point
- 1px is very close to object
- 2000px is basically flat and minimal change
rotate3d(x,y,z,m deg) m is how many degrees each rotated by and xyz are which ones do you want to rotate
translateZ is how much object is translated out of root on Z plane. usually used with rotations
-webkit-transform-origin by default is 50% 50%;
- by default rotation happens in the midde
- 50% 100% object will rotate on middle x plane and top of y plane
